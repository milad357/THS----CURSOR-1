  COPY LOGO FILE USING FINDER (2 MINUTES)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Since Terminal can't access the Documents folder, use Finder:

STEP 1: I've opened two Finder windows for you:
   • Documents folder (your logo file)
   • public folder (destination)

STEP 2: Copy the file:
   1. In Documents window, click on "Final Files-02.jpg"
   2. Press Cmd+C (or right-click → Copy)
   
STEP 3: Paste 3 times in public folder:
   1. Click in the public folder window
   2. Press Cmd+V (first copy)
   3. Press Cmd+V again (second copy)
   4. Press Cmd+V again (third copy)

STEP 4: Rename the files:
   1. Click on the first copy → Press Enter → Type: logo-light.png
   2. Click on the second copy → Press Enter → Type: logo-gray.png
   3. Click on the third copy → Press Enter → Type: logo-dark.png

STEP 5: Refresh your browser:
   • Press Cmd+Shift+R (hard refresh)
   • The logo should appear!

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

That's it! The logo will show up automatically.
